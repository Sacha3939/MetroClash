# Game Design Document (GDD)

## Game Title: MetroClash

### Game Concept
MetroClash is a strategic multiplayer game set in a post-apocalyptic urban environment where players must build, upgrade, and defend their territories while engaging in tactical battles against other players. The goal is to dominate the cityscape by controlling resources, constructing powerful buildings, and commanding various units.

### Game Mechanics
1. **Base Building**: Players construct a variety of buildings that provide different benefits, such as resource generation, unit production, or defensive capabilities.
   - **Types of Buildings**:
     - Resource Generators (e.g., Water Plant, Power Station)
     - Defensive Structures (e.g., Walls, Turrets)
     - Unit Factories
     - Research Centers
   
2. **Unit Management**: Players can create and manage different units with unique abilities and roles (e.g., Attackers, Defenders, Support).
   - **Unit Types**:
     - Infantries
     - Vehicles
     - Air Units
     - Special Units (e.g., Stealth Operations)

3. **Resource Management**: Players collect and manage resources like Food, Water, Energy, and Metal, which are essential for building and upgrading units and structures.

4. **Combat System**: An engaging and tactical combat system where players can utilize terrain advantages and unit types to outmaneuver opponents.
   - Real-time strategy elements with pause functionality to strategize moves.

### Progression Systems
- **Leveling Up**: Players gain experience points (XP) through battles and resource collection, allowing them to level up and unlock new technologies and buildings.
- **Research and Upgrades**: Players can research new technologies to improve existing buildings and units or unlock advanced structures.
- **Achievements**: Players earn achievements for reaching milestones, which can provide bonuses or unlock unique items.

### Buildings
- **Headquarters**: The central building representing the player's base. Upgrading gives access to new features.
- **Barracks**: Train infantry units.
- **Factory**: Produce vehicles and advanced units.
- **Power Plant**: Generates energy for base operations.
- **Research Lab**: Facilitates technological advancements.

### Units
1. **Scout**: Fast unit for reconnaissance and scouting enemy bases.
2. **Soldier**: Basic infantry unit for ground combat.
3. **Tank**: Heavy vehicle with high durability.
4. **Helicopter**: Air unit providing mobility and tactical advantages.
5. **Engineer**: Unit capable of repairing buildings and units in combat.

### Resources
- **Food**: Required for unit training and sustaining inhabitants.
- **Water**: Essential for survival and base operations.
- **Energy**: Powers buildings, units, and special abilities.
- **Metal**: Used for constructing and upgrading buildings and units.

### Monetization
- **In-Game Currency**: Players can purchase in-game currency to speed up processes, obtain resources, or buy exclusive builds.
- **Cosmetic Items**: Players can personalize their units or bases with skins and decorations.
- **Battle Pass**: A seasonal pass offering exclusive rewards and challenges.
- **Advertisements**: Optional ads that players can view to gain minor in-game rewards.

## Conclusion
MetroClash combines strategic gameplay with engaging mechanics, resource management, and player interaction, aiming to create a compelling and dynamic gaming experience for players who enjoy strategy games.
